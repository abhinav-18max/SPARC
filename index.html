Student Professional Awareness Regional Conclave

About

SPARC (Student Professional Awareness Regional Conclave), the third edition of SPAAM (Student Professional Awareness Activities Month) is an award-winning national-level flagship event powered by IEEE MACE SB. With around 120 delegates expected this year, SPARC will be a three-day long offline event packed with technical workshops, computer geeks, inspirational speakers, and many more exuberant activities in store for aspiring minds. A one-month-long online pre-event involving exciting competitions and workshops is also included as a part of SPARC to gear up for the experience. SPAAMâ€™21 earned global recognition by winning the prestigious Darrel Chong Student Activity Award in the silver category and was also selected as Outstanding Student Event (Special Mention) in the IEEE Kerala Section Awards 2021, for its immense contribution toward enhancing professional awareness, and technical and soft skills in emerging youth for a competitive world. Being professionally aware can help every individual in making wise decisions regarding their professional life and thus make the best out of their skills and interests. So, come join us on the 16th, 17th, and 18th of September 2022 to accentuate a brighter and more excelled version of you for the future of a better living world.
So bide your time for SPARC!!
